Goldfish

Goldfish are freshwater fish in the family Cyprinidae.It is popularly kept as a pet in indoor aquariums and one of the most popular aquarium fish

Care

Here are some tips:

  • Perform regular water changes
  • Keep your goldfish tank clean
  • Avoid overfeeding your fish
  • Quarantine new fish
  • Provide a suitably sized fish tank

Lifespan

Up to 10 years

Breeds/Types

Types of goldfish are:

  • Comet
  • Fantail
  • Veiltail
  • Pompom
  • Bubble eye
  • Oranda
  • Pearlscale
  • Ranchu

Rabbits

Rabbits are small mammals in the family Leporidea,which includes 40 or so species of rabbits,hares and Pikas.They are cute furry little creatures that most people think only like carrots but there's more to them that meets the eye.

Breeds

Here are some rabbit breeds:

  • American rabbit
  • American fuzzy lop
  • Blanc de Hotot
  • Dwarf Hotot
  • Cinnamon
  • Jersey Wooley

Diet

Rabbits don't just carrots.Here are some other things they eat:

  • Hay
  • Bunny pellets
  • Fruits
  • Vegetables
  • Rabbit treats

Facts

Here are some things you should know about rabbits:

  • They're teeth never stop growing!
  • Rabbits are born without fur and with closed eyes.
  • They live in warrens- a series of tunnels and rooms that they dig underground.

Caring guide

Here are some key steps to take when bringing home a rabbit:

  • Always feed them greens
  • Set up an indoor inclosure
  • Litter train your rabbit
  • Bunny proof your home
  • Get enrichment toys for your rabbit
  • Groom your rabbit
  • Socialize your rabbit
  • Have a rabbit veterinarian

Lifespan

Most rabbits live up to 12 years depending on their breeds,diet and overall care.
